A lightweight Python application built for the **Vibe2Ship Hackathon** by Coding Ninjas x Google for Developers. 

**CineVibe** takes a natural language "vibe" or mood from the user and leverages **Google AI Studio (Gemini 1.5 Flash)** to instantly recommend the perfect movie, complete with a genre breakdown and a tailored pitch explaining why it fits.

---

## 🚀 Why CineVibe?

The theme of Vibe2Ship is **"Vibe Coding"**—turning ideas into real products instantly using AI. While many projects focus on complex, heavy applications, **CineVibe** focuses on clean execution and a common real-world dilemma: *spending more time scrolling through streaming apps trying to choose a movie than actually watching it.* Instead of searching by strict categories, users can simply type their mood (e.g., *"A fast-paced psychological thriller to watch with friends"* or *"A relaxing, lighthearted comedy after a long day of exams"*) and get an immediate, intelligent recommendation.

---

## 🛠️ Features

- **Vibe-Based Discovery:** Move beyond standard genres; input complex descriptions of your current mood or setting.
- **Powered by Google AI Studio:** Uses the highly efficient `gemini-1.5-flash` model for instant responses.
- **Zero-Breeze Execution:** Under 15 lines of highly readable, clean Python code—no complex loops, databases, or heavy configurations.
